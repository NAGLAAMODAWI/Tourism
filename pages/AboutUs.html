About Us

It is a Sudanese youth initiative concerned with the tourism sector in an attempt to reflect the tourist areas in Sudan and focus on showing the beauty of these areas and directing the media to them with a focus on clarifying the importance of tourism as a basic economic resource of the pillars of the national economy

Our Vision

Before we started as a business, the idea started from a graduation project with the application of virtual reality technology to support and raise the level of promotion of internal tourism in Sudan through making virtual tours to the most prominent tourist places.

Our Goals

Our first and last goal is to raise the level of tourism in Sudan and focus on highlighting all the aesthetic aspects in Sudan and drawing the attention of the media side to display the tourist areas.

We Are Happy To Help You

We at SUDAN-TO are very happy to communicate with us and provide all your suggestions in order to improve the quality of the services provided
